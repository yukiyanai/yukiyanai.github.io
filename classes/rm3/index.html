Research Methods in Political Science III（政治学方法論 III）

School of Law and Graduate School of Law, Kobe University, Spring 2016


Syllabus


Overview and Class Goals

This course introduces students to causal inference based on the potential outcome approach. The students will learn some statistical methods to find out causal effects in political phenomena so that they will be able to make causal claims in their own research.
