Research Methods in Political Science II（政治学方法論 II）

School of Law and Graduate School of Law, Kobe University, Fall 2016


Syllabus


Overview and Class Goals

This is the second course of the sequence of research methods in political science at [Graduate] School of Law at Kobe University. In the first course, you learned how to conduct reproducible research with simple statistical models. In this course, you will learn more advanced statistical models that are essential for political science research.
